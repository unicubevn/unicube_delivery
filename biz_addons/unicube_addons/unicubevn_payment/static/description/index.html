UniCube's Dynamic VietQR for online payment with callback API supported by TIMO (A digital bank)

This addon will change the classic bank transfer module to Dynamic VietQR payment.

More than that, the module will create an IPN gateway to listen the callback from TIMO which provide the payment status for finishing the order's payment

Installation

  • To install this module, go to App/"UniCube Dynamic VietQR with callback supported by TIMO" then press install

  • Note: This module is depended on the "Payment: Vietnamese Bank Base Module" module. You can download it here.

Configuration

  • Step 1: Go to Setting/Website/Shop-Payment then click on "View Alternatives' button, the payment provider screen show up and the "Wire transfer" payment method had been change to "UniCube VietQR" payment provider:

  • Step 2: Then click on the "UniCube VietQR" payment provider, the detail screen will show up as below. Click on "the arrow" on the end of Payment Journal line to config the Payment Journal

  • Step 3: On the Payment Journal screen, 'the Currency' mus be 'VND' and add your bank account information into Payment Journal screen

  • Step 4: Then, click on the 'Send to TIMO' button to register your IPN URL and bank account; and TIMO will call you for further instructions and testing.

  • Step 5: After finish testing with TIMO and golive, you can receive the callback from TIMO for auto finishing the bill payment.

Usage

To use this module, you need to complete installation and configuration parts.
